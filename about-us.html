What began as a small workshop has evolved into a distinguished global apparel brand, trusted by organizations that value precision, quality, and refined design. Our Story

TIMRAX’s journey is rooted in craftsmanship and guided by an uncompromising pursuit of excellence. From the earliest days, our focus has been on perfecting the art of garment making—balancing aesthetics with functionality, and innovation with tradition.

Through continuous refinement, advanced production techniques, and a deep understanding of professional environments, TIMRAX has grown into a brand recognized for consistency, sophistication, and reliability. Every stage of our evolution reflects our commitment to setting higher standards in premium apparel manufacturing.

Our Philosophy

At TIMRAX, we believe apparel is an extension of identity. A uniform should not only represent a role, but also convey confidence, dignity, and professionalism. Our designs are created to support performance while maintaining an elegant, contemporary aesthetic.
